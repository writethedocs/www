# Sponsorship Activations

In addition to our [standard sponsorship packages](/conf/{{shortcode}}/{{year}}/sponsors/prospectus/), we offer activation opportunities that give sponsors a more experiential presence at the conference. All activations can be customized — reach out to <sponsorship@writethedocs.org> to discuss.

## Writing Day Sponsorship

Sponsor branding at Writing Day, the opening day of the conference where attendees collaborate on documentation projects. Writing Day draws about one third of total conference attendance.

- **Time:** 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM, Sunday
- **Location:** Conference venue

### What Write the Docs Provides

- Entrance signage with sponsor logo
- Sponsor logo on Writing Day program materials
- Acknowledgment during Writing Day opening remarks
- Table for sponsor collateral

### What the Sponsor Provides

- Stickers, flyers, or other small collateral for the table

---

### What Write the Docs Provides

- Table for sponsor collateral

- Stickers, flyers, or other small collateral for the table

## Branded Food & Dessert Station

An optional add-on for social event sponsorships. A branded food or dessert station (e.g., ice cream booth) at the Sunday Welcome Reception or Monday Evening Social, with dedicated signage featuring the sponsor logo.

- **Time:** During the sponsored social event
- **Location:** Social event venue

### What Write the Docs Provides

- Food or dessert vendor and service
- Dessert station signage with sponsor logo
- "Hosted by [Sponsor]" signage with sponsor logo

## Martha's Takeover

A sponsor takeover of the Martha's coffee shop space during the morning unconference block. Attendees get free handcrafted beverages (espresso, matcha, or other specialty drinks), and the sponsor hosts short discussion sessions in the space. This activation overlaps with the morning coffee break.

- **Time:** 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM (conference days)
- **Location:** Martha's (adjacent to unconference rooms)

### What Write the Docs Provides

- Martha's venue space, reserved and set up
- Beverage service: professional baristas, equipment, carts, cups, and supplies for the full 2-hour window
- Custom branded cups and carts with sponsor branding
- Wayfinding and entrance signage with sponsor logo
- Promotion in conference program and schedule

### What the Sponsor Provides

- Roll-up banners (up to 2) and posters/signs (up to 4, max 24" x 36")
- Stickers and swag items for tables
- Staff to facilitate discussion sessions

### Discussion Sessions

The sponsor may host up to three 30-minute blocks during the activation window. These can include product demos, topic discussions, interviews, or other interactive formats. Topics should be submitted 2 weeks before the event for program inclusion.

## Attendee Lounge

A sponsor-branded wellness and recharging space where attendees can take a break. Features comfortable seating, light refreshments, and a quiet atmosphere. Open throughout the conference.

- **Location:** Dedicated lounge space at the venue
- **Availability:** Monday and Tuesday, full conference hours

### What Write the Docs Provides

- Lounge furniture: mix of seating, lounge chairs, sofas, rugs, and high-top tables
- Plants and greenery
- Water and light snack station
- Entrance signage with sponsor logo

### What the Sponsor Provides

- Sign (1, 24" x 36") for display within the lounge
- Stickers and postcards for tables
- Non-alcoholic beverages and container (optional)

---

## Professional Headshot Booth

Free professional headshots for all attendees, sponsored by your company. Sponsor branding appears on surrounding signage, with an optional table adjacent to the booth. Photos do not include sponsor logos.

- **Location:** Designated area near main conference space

### What Write the Docs Provides

- Professional photographer
- Wayfinding and booth signage with sponsor logo
- 6-foot table adjacent to the photo booth (optional)

### What the Sponsor Provides

- Table sign (optional, up to 24" x 36")
- Table collateral (optional) — swag, devices, or printed materials

---

## Co-Branded Swag

A useful, co-branded item distributed to all attendees featuring both the Write the Docs and sponsor logos. Items should be practical and conference-appropriate (e.g., cable organizers, portable chargers). Write the Docs manages production and distribution.

- **Quantity:** All registered attendees (~{{ about.attendees }})

### What Write the Docs Provides

- Swag item sourcing and production coordination
- Co-branding with WTD and sponsor logos
- Distribution to all attendees

### What the Sponsor Provides

- Approval of swag item proof before production (4 weeks before event)
- Additional swag items for distribution (optional, coordinate with WTD)

## General Logistics

### Signage

- All WTD-produced signage is designed by Write the Docs and includes the sponsor logo
- Sponsor-provided signage should be professional quality (foam board, vinyl banner, or roll-up) and approved by WTD for size and placement

### Shipping

- **Ship-to address:** Provided closer to the event. The venue does not accept packages directly.
- **Shipping window:** Materials must arrive between April 27 and May 1.
- Sponsors are responsible for return shipping. Print return labels before arriving at the venue.
- **All materials must be out of the venue by 5:30 PM on Tuesday.**

### Contact

For questions about activations or to customize a package:

- <sponsorship@writethedocs.org>
