## Getting There

bUm Berlin is easily accessible by public transport. The following stops are within a 15-minute walk:

**Bus**

- Line M29: Ohlauer Str.
- Line 194: Pflügerstr.
- Line M41: Jahnstr.

**U-Bahn**

- U8: Schönleinstraße
- U1 and U3: Görlitzer Bahnhof
