Write the Docs is a global community of people who care about documentation, supported by a small organization that exists to serve the community.

Guiding Principles and Governance
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Our :doc:`/about-us` page describes the principles that guide us as a community organization — community-first, sustainability, openness, and inclusivity — as well as the legal and business structure that helps us support and build the community.

The Team
~~~~~~~~

Write the Docs wouldn't be possible without our wonderful :doc:`/team`. In addition to a small team of paid staff, we have volunteers who help out across many areas of the community.

We're always looking for folks who want to take a stronger leadership role. Feel free to reach out if you're interested in helping on a conference or in the community in general.

Origin Story
~~~~~~~~~~~~

If you're new to the community and curious about our history, check out the :doc:`/origin-story`. You can also read about how we came to call ourselves :doc:`/documentarians`.

Code of Conduct
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

We believe strongly in providing a welcoming and inclusive community. Our :doc:`/code-of-conduct` describes what we expect in our online and in-person spaces.
